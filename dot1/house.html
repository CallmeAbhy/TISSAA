 Vision & Mission

The primary goal of TISSAA is to support TISS alums for their personal and professional development, who will, in turn, help our institute TISS as well as society at large. TISSAA not only aims to bring together like-minded TISSians close but is open to all TISS graduates and offers a broader networking scope.

We are planning strategic and long-range programs to promote Alums awareness, engagement and commitment to the Institute.
